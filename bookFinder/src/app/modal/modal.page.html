To find your book’s call number and collection:


1. Go to https://library.tcnj.edu/



2. Enter your book’s name into the search bar.



3. Select Books & Media and search.



4. Locate the book you’re looking for from the list of results.



5. If the book is listed as “Available at Gitenstein Library” the collection and call number will be listed afterwards.



Note: Do NOT include parentheses in the call number.
